About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles. We bridge the gap between academic insight—through our UK university partnerships—and real-world projects in high-consequence environments.

Our Core Model

Academy

Specialized training and bootcamps for students and professionals to master digital risk.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing intellectual property, future risk models, and robust AI governance frameworks.

Advisory Services

Helping organizations implement critical frameworks like NIST, ISO 27001, and NIS2.

Who We Serve

Our community includes students, early-career professionals, and practitioners in sectors like financial services and critical infrastructure seeking upskilling in digital and cyber risk.
